<h2>Bio</h2>
<p>Hi, my name is Wushi Dong. I am a graduating physics PhD student at The University of Chicago. My PhD research is focused on modeling quantum-scale electron transport in 2D semiconductors for next-generation chip design. To perform such calculations based on quantum field theory, I developed my own simulation software in C++ utilizing high-performance computing techniques. Besides, I gained my machine learning experience from developing brain-inspired deep learning algorithms for video action recognition during a research internship at IBM. Equipped with knowledge of both hardware and software, I am passionate about transforming modern computing power into real-world applications.
</p>
